1952—How to Make Hats
by Ruby Carnahan

FABRICS USED TO COVER FRAMES AND FOUNDATIONS

  • VELVET
  • MOIRE
  • SATIN
  • CREPE
  • JERSEY
  • STRAW CLOTH
  • BROCADE
  • TWEED
  • LAME
  • WOOLEN MATERIALS
  • TAFFETA
  • COTTON MATERIALS

ALL OF THE ABOVE MATERIALS AND MANY MORE ARE USED TO COVER FRAMES AND FOUNDATIONS.

VELVET HAS A NAP. ALWAYS CUT EACH PIECE OF VELVET WITH THE NAP RUNNING IN THE SAME DIRECTION.

SATIN AND MANY OTHER MATERIALS THAT HAVE A SHEEN SHOULD BE CUT WITH EACH PIECE RUNNING THE SAME WAY, TO PREVENT MATERIAL COLORS FROM APPEARING DIFFERENT.

INSTRUCTIONS FOR COVERING FRAMES AND FOUNDATIONS ARE GIVEN ON FOLLOWING PAGES.
