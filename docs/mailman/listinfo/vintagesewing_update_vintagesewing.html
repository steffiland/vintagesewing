Vintagesewing_update -- Announcement of new works published at VintageSewing.info

 

About Vintagesewing_update
English (USA)

Announcement list - not a discussion list.

This list is used to notify subscribers when a new work is published to VintageSewing.info.

To see the collection of prior postings to the list, visit the Vintagesewing_update Archives.

Using Vintagesewing_update
To post a message to all the list members, send email to vintagesewing_update@vintagesewing.info.

You can subscribe to the list, or change your existing subscription, in the sections below.
